# Reach The Launch — Client Guide

Welcome to your new website, **Reach The Launch** — the real estate platform dedicated to showcasing the best current property launches in the Egyptian market (East Cairo, West Cairo, North Coast, and Ain Sokhna).

This guide walks you through using your dashboard and running the site day-to-day, with no technical background needed.

---

## 1) User Levels on the Site

| User | Can do |
|---|---|
| **Guest (visitor)** | Browse projects and developers only |
| **Client** (a buyer — creates an account to inquire about a launch) | Browse + submit their details to express interest in a project. **Cannot edit anything on the site** — this is purely the person shopping for a property |
| **Admin** | Only the specific sections you've switched on for them (see section 6) |
| **You (Owner / Super Admin)** | Everything — plus full control over exactly what each Admin can access |

> **Note:** "Client" here always means someone shopping for a property to buy — never someone who manages or edits the website.

---

## 2) How to Access Your Dashboard

1. Open the website and sign in with your account (the one you own the site with)
2. You'll see "**Admin Panel**" at the top of the site — click it
3. You'll land on an Overview screen showing every section you have access to

---

## 3) How to Add a New Developer Company

Before adding any project, the developer company behind it needs to be added first.

1. From the dashboard, click the "**Developers**" tab
2. On the left you'll see a form: enter the company name, a logo image link (if available), and a short description (optional)
3. Click "**Add Developer**"
4. The company appears immediately in the list on the right, and you can edit or delete it anytime from the same page

---

## 4) How to Add a New Launch (Project)

1. From the dashboard, click the "**Add Launch**" tab
2. Fill in the details:
   - Project name
   - Select the developer company (from the ones you've already added)
   - Select the region (East / West / North Coast / Sokhna)
   - Cover image link, plus optional gallery image links (separated by commas)
   - Starting price
   - Launch window (e.g. August 2026)
   - "Best Time to Buy" — the highlighted text shown on the project page (e.g. "Register in Phase 1 before official pricing is released")
   - Available unit types (apartment, duplex, villa, etc.)
   - A detailed project description
3. Click "**Create Launch**"
4. The project appears immediately on the homepage and carousel — no extra steps needed

**To edit or delete an existing project:** go to the "**Launches**" tab in the dashboard, where you'll find "Edit" and "Delete" buttons next to each project.

---

## 5) Homepage Special Offer Banner

A highlighted promotional section that appears on the homepage right under the carousel — useful for time-limited deals, discounts, or announcements.

1. From the dashboard, click "**Special Offer**"
2. Fill in a title (required), an optional description, an optional image link, and an optional button (text + link)
3. Click "**Publish Offer**" — it appears on the homepage immediately
4. To update it later, just change the fields and click "**Save Changes**"
5. Click "**Remove from Homepage**" anytime — the entire section disappears from the homepage instantly, no empty gap left behind

---

## 6) How to Give Someone Else "Admin" Access — With Exact Permissions

This is for someone helping *you* manage the site — not a buyer. You control precisely what each admin can touch; nothing is granted automatically.

1. Ask them to first create a regular Client account on the site (Create Account) — the same signup a buyer would use
2. From your dashboard, click the "**Manage Admins**" tab (only visible to you, the Super Admin)
3. You'll see a list of all registered accounts
4. Next to their name, click "**Make Admin**" — at this point they have zero permissions, just the "Admin" label
5. Tick exactly which sections they're allowed to use:
   - **Launches** — add/edit/delete projects
   - **Developers** — add/edit/delete developer companies
   - **Special Offer** — set or remove the homepage banner
   - **Contact Submissions** — view, mark done, and delete client inquiries
6. They'll only see the tabs matching what you've ticked — everything else stays hidden and blocked, even if they try the address directly
7. Untick any box anytime to take that permission back, or click "**Revoke Admin**" to remove them entirely

---

## 7) How to View and Manage Client "Contact Us" Submissions

These are the buyers who reached out because they're interested in purchasing.

1. From the dashboard, click the "**Contact Submissions**" tab
2. You'll see a numbered table (#1 is the very first submission you ever received) with everyone's details: name, phone number, unit type they're interested in, preferred date (if provided), and which project they're asking about (or "General Inquiry" if it wasn't tied to a specific project)
3. Once you've followed up with someone, click "**Mark Done**" — their row gets struck through so you can see at a glance who's still pending. Click "**Undo**" to bring it back
4. Click "**Delete**" to permanently remove a submission you no longer need

---

## 8) General Tips

- Use high-quality, clear cover images for every project — it's the first thing a buyer sees
- Make "Best Time to Buy" specific and urgent (dates, discounts, booking phases) — that's the core idea of the site
- Check the "Contact Submissions" tab daily so you don't miss an interested buyer
- Once a project's launch window has passed, you can edit or remove it anytime from the "Launches" tab
- Keep the Special Offer banner current — remove it the moment a promotion ends so the homepage never shows outdated deals
